Our Story

A Quarter Century of
Italian Excellence

Since 1998, La Terrazza has defined Italian dining in Vancouver. Heirloom family recipes meet seasonal West-Coast produce, while our award-winning cellar of Italian wines ensures every dish finds its perfect partner.

Nestled in the heart of Yaletown, our kitchen honours the traditions of the Italian table — where the finest ingredients speak for themselves and every meal becomes an occasion.

— La Famiglia
